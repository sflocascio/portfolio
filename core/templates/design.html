  5. User Testing

{% comment %} Business Directory {% endcomment %}

Perform research through series of usability and accessibility tests , evaluate design with multiple user scenarios.


  6. Iteration


{% comment %} Business Directory {% endcomment %}

Redo the entire process and use new insights to improve on the product’s design and user experience.



“Something that looks great but is difficult to use is exemplary of great UI and poor UX. While Something very usable that looks terrible is exemplary of great UX and poor UI.”

This is one of my favorite expressions of UI and UX. My goal and approach with UI and UX is to connect business goals with user needs through a process of testing and refinement that leads to a product that satisfies both sides of the relationship.

In other words, my ultimate goal is to make something that looks great and is very usable.
