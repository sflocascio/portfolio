The Little Triangle

a web app built to offer parents in North Carolina’s Triangle Region a platform to discover, review, and gain valuable insights about the best activities, camps, and businesses in their area.
{% comment %}
{% endcomment %}

The Problem

All parents have faced it. You see a new exciting camp, class, or activity out there and you want to sign your kid up for it. It seems like a great idea but once you start going through the registration process these questions come to mind :

Is it safe? Is it worth the money? Is it worth the time?

The concept for the little Triangle was created by such a parent, seeking to give parents in her region a voice and platform for sharing insights about these important things. .
